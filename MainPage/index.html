What is free fire?

Garena Free Fire (also known as Free Fire) is a battle royale game, developed by 111dots Studio and published by Garena INTERNATIONAL for Android and iOS. It became the most downloaded mobile game globally in 2021. Due to its popularity, the game received the award for the "Best Popular Vote Game" by the Google Play Store in 2020.
